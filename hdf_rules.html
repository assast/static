Complete download of a torrent followed by a minimum of seeding for 72 hours (seeding less than 72 hours is not counted by the system as indicated in the ratio rule)

creation of posts on the forum (simple answers not being sufficient answer yes no ok hello welcome ....... does not constitute sufficient activity for the site).

    => 2) Using freeleech tokens. :



    What is a Freeleech token for?

    A Freeleech token allows you to download a torrent without its ratio being affected.
    On the other hand, if we leave the torrent shared, the seed is counted.
    So it's all good and you absolutely have to use them to build a good ratio!

    How many tokens do I have?

    The number of tokens remaining is indicated in the bar at the top, next to the required ratio.
    It is also indicated in the member page in the "statistics" section.

    How to use a Freeleech token?

    - Go to the page of the torrent you want to download.
    - Then, to download the .torrent file, do not use the T button as usual, but the FL button.
    - The site asks for confirmation, click on OK.

    Freeleech Token Notes and Limitations:

    - A Freeleech token can only be used once. If the .torrent file is opened again to download the file, the ratio will be counted.
    - A Freeleech token is personal and cannot be transferred or used by another account.
    - A Freeleech token expires after 10 days, so do not save the .torrent file to download it later otherwise it will no longer be valid. You must start the download immediately.
    - Be careful to check that the torrent on which you are using a token has seed, otherwise the file will never arrive and you will have lost a token.

    The staff won't get you out of ratio problems if you don't use your Tokens!

Ratio rules


=> "Ratio:



  • The ratio is calculated by dividing your total uploaded data by your total uploaded data. You can see this in the header of the site or in the statistics part of your profile.
  • To maintain your download privileges, your ratio must remain above a certain value. This minimum value is the required ratio.
  • If your ratio drops below the required ratio, you will have two weeks to bring your ratio back above the required ratio. During this period you will be in ratio monitoring.
  • If you fail to reach the required ratio within the time limit, you will no longer be able to download. However, your account will still be active.

    • Warning:

    • Donating to the site will not get you out of watch ratio and will not restore the right to download.
    • For this you can send a PM to the staff who may, depending on the situation, consider granting you the right to upload in order to raise your ratio.
    The seedtime (Minimum mandatory sharing time) per torrent is 72H.
    A torrent that is shared less than 72 hours in the last week will not be considered.


    => Required Ratio:



    • The required ratio represents the minimum ratio you must maintain to not be on ratio watch. You can see your required ratio in the header of the site or in the statistics part of your profile.
    • Your required ratio is unique, it is calculated for each account specifically.
    • Your required ratio is calculated from (1) the total data you uploaded and (2) the total number of torrents you uploaded, but is not limited to your uploaded torrents.
    • The ratio system reduces your required ratio when you send a large number of torrents. The more torrents you upload, the lower your required ratio will be and therefore you will be less likely to be on ratio watch.


    => Table of required ratios

    => The seedtime (Minimum mandatory sharing time) per torrent is 72H.



    Calculation of the required ratio:
    • 1: Determine your required ratio values: In the table above the row with gray background defines the required ratio values.
    • 2: Determine your current required ratio. Your current required ratio is a number between the maximum and minimum required ratio value that you determined in the previous step. To determine the actual required ratio value, the system first takes the maximum required ratio value and multiplies it by [1 − (torrents uploaded / torrents completed)]. Another representation of this calculation:



      • In this calculation, torrents completed is the number of torrents you have completed without deleting them. If you complete the same torrent twice, it will only be counted once. If you delete a completed torrent, it won't count.
      • In this calculation, shared torrents is the average number of torrents you have seeded over 72 hours in the last week. If a torrent has been shared for less than 72 hours in the last week, it will not be included in this total.
    • 3: Higher value if necessary. When the previously calculated value falls below the minimum required ratio, the minimum required ratio is then taken rather than the calculated value (Indeed the calculated value can be equal to 0 when the number of uploaded torrents is equal to the number of completed torrents).


    => Details:



    • If you stop uploading for a week, your required ratio will be equal to your maximum ratio. After resuming the upload for a period of 72 hours, your required ratio will decrease according to the previous calculation method.
    • If your downloaded total is less than 25 GB you cannot be in ratio monitoring. In these circumstances, you will not have a required ratio.
    • If your downloaded total is less than 20 Gb and the number of completed torrents matches the number of seeded torrents, your ratio will be 0.


    => Examples:



    • In this example, AKV downloaded 25 GB. It is therefore in the 20-30 GB range. Its maximum required ratio is therefore 0.30 and its minimum required ratio is therefore 0.05.
    • In this example, AKV has 90 torrents completed, and is currently seeded to 45 torrents.
    • To calculate AKV's current required ratio, we take its maximum required ratio, which is 0.30, and multiply it by [1 - (torrents uploaded / torrents completed)] (which is 0.50). Which give: 0.30 * [1 - (45 / 90)] = 0.15
    • The resulting ratio is therefore 0.15, which is between the maximum ratio of 0.30 and the minimum ratio of 0.05. The minimum ratio of AKV is therefore 0.15.


    => Ratio Watch:



    • You must have downloaded at least 5 Gb for ratio monitoring to start.
    • If you are on ratio monitoring, you have 2 weeks to reach your required ratio.
    • If you downloaded more than 10 GB while in ratio monitoring, you will not be able to download anymore.
    • If you can't leave ratio monitoring for two weeks, you won't be able to download anymore. However, your account remains active.
    • Ratio monitoring is automatic and cannot be stopped by staff.


    => Exit ratio monitoring:



    • To exit ratio monitoring, you must either upload torrents or seed more completed torrents. Your ratio must be equal to or greater than the required ratio, to exit ratio monitoring.
    • You will be able to download again when your ratio becomes higher than the required ratio.
