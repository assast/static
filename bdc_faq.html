What is "Ratio"?

Ratio; It is the numerical result obtained by mathematically dividing the amount of sending in a torrent file by the amount of download. For example, if you downloaded 100 MB in a torrent and sent 200 MB, your ratio ratio for this torrent is 2. (200/100=2)

You must provide at least 0.8 ratio in a torrent on our site. (if this is not possible, you should seed for at least 5 days). Note; These are the minimum rates. It will be in your best interest to exceed these rates as much as you can.

Also, your overall ratio should not fall below 0.8. Otherwise, if you get a warning and don't fix it, you will face download ban after 2 weeks. For Ratio increasing methods click here

How do I clear cookies?

You can clear all your cookies by clicking here . If you return to the main index page via the provided link and are still logged in, you may need to manually remove your cookies.

On Windows:

Click the "Tools" menu.
Select "Internet Options" from the menu that appears.
Click "Delete Cookies" in the dialog that appears. It will be in the middle area of ​​the "General" tab.


In Mozilla Firefox:

Click the "Tools" menu.
Select "Options" from the menu that appears.
Select the "Privacy" option on the left from the dialog box.
Find "Cookies" in the main pane and click the adjacent "Clear" button.
Your cookies should be removed. You may want to restart the computer and visit the site again.
