uTorrent

Create Contribute History
µTorrent is a torrent client, i.e. software capable of interconnecting thousands of computers in order to share files via the peer protocol to Peer (P2P) BitTorrent.
The software is signed by the designer of the protocol BitTorrent  ;and software of the same name; first official software dedicated to the sharing network. His particuliarity ? Being lighter and easier to use.
AlthoughµTorrentweighs only 1MB and has a relatively clean interface, that doesn't mean it lacks features.
Of course, it has the minimum functions required for such software: adding .torrent files, sharing, managing magnet links, managing files and priorities, but not only. This includes, for example, a configurable search engine.
µTorrent can also manage RSS feeds for you so you don't miss any of your download feeds.
The software can also be controlled remotely either by its smartphone application or via a web interface. Convenient to launch downloads on your computer as soon as you have the idea.
The installation process of µTorrent (uTorrent) includes an automatic download module for the French translation.

The Site Dictionary

Create
BITRATE:
A measure of the amount of digital data transmitted per unit time.
It is most often expressed in bits per second (bps) and kilobits/s (kbps) per 1000 bps.
For a film, we speak of a total bitrate (ex: 9610 Kbps) as the sum of the video bitrate (example: 8000 Kbps) and the audio bitrate (ex: 1610 Kbps).
It is an indicator of the quality of a release (the higher the bitrate, the higher the quality expected of a release)

BITTORRENT:
The Bittorrent protocol is a process for exchanging "big" data between several people.
To put these people in contact you need what is called a tracker (site that centralizes the .torrent files necessary for exchanges) such as HD-F.

CUSTOMER:
In a computer network a client is the principle that sends requests to a server. It can be software manipulated by a person, or a bot. The client can be a computer, software or computing device and as such depends on a server.

CODEC:
Codec comes from compression-decompression or coding-decoding (COde-DECode in English). A codec is a process capable of compressing and/or decompressing a digital signal.
On the one hand, codecs encode streams or signals for data transmission, storage or encryption. On the other hand, they decode these streams or signals for editing or playback. For example, MPEG-4 AVC/H.264 is a video standard, and x264 is a codec capable of producing a video stream adhering to this standard.

Example of Audio Codecs allowed on HDF:
AC-3, DTS, TrueHD, DTS-HD, PCM, ATMOS, EAC3, FLAC

Example of video Codecs allowed on HDF:
x264, x265, VC-1, AVC

CROP (CROPPING, CROPPER)
Action of cutting the black bands at the top and bottom of the image at the time of encoding to reduce the size of the release without hindering the image. Cropping is optional and is not performed by all encoders. Mainly used by US scene and trackers.

DETAG:
Action of deleting a signature, or Tag, usually found at the end of a torrent's name.
It gives information about the creator(s) of a release or a torrent.
See here forums.php?action=viewthread&threadid=367

DOWNLOAD, DL, DOWNLOADER:
Download, download.

ENCODING:
Transform the signal of a video (example: Full BluRay or Remux) to another format (ex: HDrip 1080p) to reduce the weight, reducing the quality to a minimum.

FAKE:
In French, it means False or Imposture. This means, here, that the torrent offers content that has no relation to the presentation or does not exist. For example, a torrent offering Ccleaner 10.4 is a fake since this tool does not exist in this version.

FANSUB:
Action of subtitling a video for personal and non-professional purposes. The quality of Fansubs can vary greatly (translation accuracy, mistakes, etc.) and these are called FastSubs.

FASTSUB:
Fansub made as fast as possible, at the expense of quality.

FORUM
Discussion area of ​​a site.
On HDF the forum is available here: https://hdf.world/forums.php

FREE LEECH:
This principle only exists on trackers using a ratio system. It is based on the fact that the amount of data downloaded is not counted by the tracker and therefore the ratio cannot go down. The shares remain in the accounts so, in addition to being able to download without limit, your ratio can increase. Enjoy!

FRENCH, FR:
Indicates that the proposed file is in French. For films, the indication "FRENCH" is supposed to indicate that the voices are dubbed in Quebec.

FULL BLURAY
Compliant copy of the commercial BluRay, either in image format (ISO), or in the form of a folder containing the tree structure of the BluRay. It is the equivalent of a "DVD-R" but for BluRay. All tracks from the original BluRay are retained.

HADOPI:
High Authority for the dissemination of works and the protection of rights on the Internet. It is an independent public authority and enjoys legal personality.
Its mission is to promote the development of the legal offer and to observe the legal and illegal use of works on the Internet, to protect works against infringements of the rights attached to them and to regulate the use technical protection measures.

HD (High Definition):
On the site, the term HD (High Definition) indicates videos whose number of lines (vertical definition or height) is greater than or equal to 720 or whose number of columns (horizontal definition or width) is greater than or equal to 1280.
There are mainly three HD categories:
- 720p or HD Ready (1280x720 in 16:9)
- 1080p or Full HD (1920x1080 in 16:9)
- 4K or Ultra HD (3840x2160 in 16:9).

HDRip:
This is a "High Definition" Rip of a Bluray.

FREELEECH TOKEN:
Way on the site to download a torrent without the ratio being counted (see FREELEECH)

LEECH, LEECHER:
(Leech) Downloading... This refers to people who are downloading on a torrent. A leecher is also a potential seeder because the slightest data received is shared, but it will only become fully-fledged when its download is complete.

LiMiTED:
This tag means that the film had only a limited distribution in cinemas (sometimes less than 250 cinemas). Generally, these are arthouse films, or medium-length films.

mHD:
Release having an HD definition (720p or 1080p) but a so-called "light" encoding, ie at a lower bitrate than an HDrip. On HD§F mHD are authorized with upload restrictions to preserve the quality of the site.

MP or PM:
Private Message or Private Message. This is what we call the messages that are sent from one member to another within the site, via the internal messaging system.
It is a way for one user to communicate with another.

MULTi:
Tag of a release with several audio tracks in different languages ​​(at least here, the VO and the VF)

NFO:
The NFO file is a text information file whose extension (part after the . in the filename) is NFO (not txt).
An NFO file is mandatory for each upload and must contain a certain amount of information depending on the type of files uploaded.
It can be obtained using Mediainfo (for MKV) or BDinfo (for Full BluRay) software

NUKE:
Refers to a badly done release, audio/video lag, compression with too high a loss, incomplete NFO, corrupted files….

SHARING:
Make available. Being a seeder, having files accessible to others (open client).

PEERS:
Seeders or Leechers of a release are peers, they are the members who share a file between them, whether they own it in its entirety or not yet.

PEER TO PEER, P2P:
Peer-to-peer, often abbreviated “P2P” and peer-to-peer in French, is a computer network model close to the client-server model but where each client is also a server. Peer-to-peer systems allow multiple computers to communicate over a network, and most often, simply share files.
Peer-to-peer allowed a decentralization of systems, previously based on a few servers, by allowing all computers to play the role of client and server.

POST, POSTER:
Synonymous with Up and Uploader.
See the "Upload" definition

PREZ:
Abbreviation for presentation. The prez is the visible part of your upload. This is the page that appears when viewing a torrent.
A Prez can be created by a prez generator (there are many) or by hand. A prez uses BBCODE tags and must follow upload rules.

PROPER:
Is a tag that can be added in the name of a release if it has already been made by another team in a lower quality (use of another Telesync, or one from the same source, but of better quality ). This may also indicate a better translation for subtitles unlike FASTSUBs which are hasty translations.

PV:
Abbreviation for "Private".
Often used to tell someone to communicate

RATIO:

READ.NFO:
Means that the team has a message to convey, usually concerning the release with this tag.

RELEASE:
English word meaning "release" or "release" release of a film or a music album, ... Final product of a team.

REMUX
Video and audio tracks from a BluRay are "multiplexed" into a .mkv container without undergoing re-encoding, which maintains the same quality as Blu-ray, while reducing weight by removing unnecessary languages , menus or bonuses

REPACK:
If a release is not good or something was missing and a correction is made that will solve the problem, it will be completely redone in Repack. It can also be a rip of a 700MB video from the 1.4GB version.

RE-TAG:
The Retag consists of uploading a file with a file name that is not the original one, which does not facilitate its classification and correction.
Example Gladiateur.1080p.MULTi.kekeflo instead of Gladiateur.1080p.MULTi.TBB
It is forbidden on HD-F but it is true that members often download already re-tagged files elsewhere to upload them here.
It is possible to do a tag search on Google Title+Format+exact size.
See here forums.php?action=viewthread&threadid=367

RIP:
This is to create a file containing for example a film recovered from the original source (dvd/bluray).
This file is usually much smaller in size, by a compression process, and requires the codec corresponding to the compression technique to play.
A BluRay rip will be the rip of a BluRay and depending on the codec used it may or may not be played on different devices.

SCRAPE:
The Bittorrent client starts with a scrape, which consists of asking the tracker for information on the total number of sources (seeds) and leeches (leech).
This number will usually be displayed in parentheses.

SEED, SEEDER:
Leaving torrents active in your bittorrent client causes them to be seeded. You are therefore considered a "Seeder" on shared torrents.

SEEDBOX:
A seedbox, in BitTorrent jargon, corresponds to a server (therefore equipment designed to operate 24 hours a day) which has a good internet connection on which a BitTorrent client is installed which will take care of "seeder", that is to say to share torrent files for you

DEDICATED SERVER:
Remote computer made available to a single client by a host and which can be managed remotely via the Internet network. It can host among other things a Seedbox, allowing to download and share torrents at a high speed and in a protected way.

SHOUTBOX:
A shoutbox (Anglicism for "discussion box") is an instant messaging system, integrated into a Web page that allows people to leave messages on the site. It is also a very fast tool, considering that the box is already ready. All that remains is to type the message and click on the send button.

SOURCE:
To be a seed and therefore to be a Seeder is to be a source. You are the source that will provide the content you share to whoever asks for it.
You are the original source, if you are sharing your own upload for the first time.

STV:
Straight To Video, a film never released in cinemas and directly on sale.

HARDSUB:
Subtitles embedded in the video that appear during passages in foreign languages. HARDSUBs are not encouraged as they cannot be removed or mined.

TAG:
The TAG is the uploader's signature. It is placed at the end of the name of this one.
Ex: The.Smurfs.2.2013.MULTI.1080p.BluRay.x264-NERDHD <- the TAG is NERDHD
It is important to respect the uploader's TAG otherwise it is a DETAG or a RETAG.

TEAM:
Regarding the Bittorrent world, it is a group of people who make their own releases and upload them themselves under their banner. These groups mostly exist to commit to providing quality.

TRACKER:
A BitTorrent tracker identifies downloaders in order to put them in contact with each other. People interested in the same content will form what is called a cluster (Swarm). The role of the tracker is then to communicate the coordinates of each user of the same cluster. This cluster is actually associated with the .torrent file, which also identifies the content.

TRUEFRENCH:
Indicated on films where the voices are dubbed in France.

UNRATED:
Uncensored version of a movie.

UPLOAD, UP, UPLOADER:
Shared file. (Up,Upload)
Send data, upload, share, seed. (Upload)
Also use to talk about the person who is sharing (the uploader).

UPSCALE:
It's taking a lower resolution video and converting it to a higher resolution video. This is prohibited on quality trackers.

VFF:
French version. Also called TRUEFRENCH.

PFD:
French International Version (when the film has only one French dubbing)

VFQ:
Quebec version. Also called FRENCH.

VOF:
Original version of the film in French (said of a French or French-speaking film)

VOSTFR:

Original Version

VPN:
In computing, a virtual private network, abbreviated as RPV in Quebec and VPN (Virtual Private Network) elsewhere, is a system for creating a direct link between remote computers.
In the world of torrenting, it is a way to hide your IP address in order to protect yourself from instances like Hadopi.

First Notions (Basics)

Create
﻿First Notions (Basics)


What's in a .torrent file?
It contains information such as the IP address of the server (or domain name) of the tracker (here HD-F) (which will relay the sharing information to other peers), and a hash fingerprint, unique to each torrent.

How do I use my .torrent?
To do this, you need a torrent client, it is this client that will process the .torrent file and retrieve the content that is bound.

What is the tracker for?
A tracker is a server that always knows which seeds and which peers are connected to torrent file. Customers regularly send him information.
In exchange, they receive information (ex: IP addresses) concerning the other clients to which they can connect. The tracker is not directly involved in the data transfer and does not own a copy of the file.

Seeders / Leechers?
The leecher is someone who is connected to the torrent but does not own the file, or not all of it.
The seeder is someone who is connected to the torrent AND owns the whole file.

Upload / Download?
Upload is the amount of data you have sent. The download is the amount of data you
downloaded.

The ratio
The ratio is calculated this way: Ratio = Upload / Download
As a member of HD-F, you have an "overall" ratio of your account (present at the top left). It is also indicated the minimum ratio required on the site. You can find more info on ratio here.

is allowed under 2 conditions.

1) The torrent has already been 100% shared by the uploader at least once.
2) The torrent was uploaded more than 72h (3 days) ago and still hasn't been shared.

Can I seed from multiple torrent clients?

Create
Can I seed for several torrent clients?


Finally, to increase your sharing speed, you may be tempted if you have several torrent clients (for example 1 on your PC and one on a Seedbox) to want to seed the torrent from both clients.

If you seed the same torrent from several torrent clients: This does not work!
If the tracker sees you seeding on the same torrent, from several different clients/ips, then the upload will be blocked.


On the other hand, it is quite possible to seed different torrents from different torrent clients.

Example:
You own the torrent Akira.1988.MULTi.1080p.BluRay.x264-LOST and Sonic.The.Hedgehog.2020.MULTi.TRUEFRENCH.1080p.BluRay.x264-BEO.
You with a torrent client A (on your PC) and a torrent client B (on your seedbox).


It is possible to seed Akira.1988.MULTi.1080p.BluRay. x264-LOST from the torrent client A and Sonic.The.Hedgehog.2020.MULTi.TRUEFRENCH.1080p.BluRay.x264-BEO from the torrent client B.


On the other hand,
it will not be possible to seed as well Akira.1988.MULTi.1080p.BluRay.x264 -LOST from the torrent client B as well, since already in seed on the < span style="color: #bf9000;">torrent client A.
It will also not be possible to seed Sonic.The.Hedgehog.2020 .MULTi.TRUEFRENCH.1080p.BluRay.x264-BEO from the torrent client A because already in seed from torrent client B.
